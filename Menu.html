Sinigang Na Hipon

Sinigang Na Hipon is a Filipino sour soup with it's main ingredient or point is Hipon, or Shrimp. It is comprised of various different vegetables including radish and onions. It is a delicately-prepared hot dish that is served with white hot rice and on cold rainy days. Shrimp is locally fished and caught in many of the famous fishing spots in the Philippines and cooked to perfection by our chef.

P500

Adobo

Adobo is a pork food with a twist of sweet, salty, and sour. It is mixable with many condiments like soy sauce, vinegar and sometimes sugar, thus allowing it to be easily adjustable to the customer. The pork is sourced from organic partnered farms.

P350

Kare-Kare

Kare-Kare is a beef meat recipe that utilizes it's selection of our quality-chosen eggplant, pechay and string beans. It is usually sweet in flavor, but it can be cooked to be less sweet if requested by our customer. Our Kare-Kare's vegetables have been grown in specialized farms to bring out their flavor and charm.

P400

Pinakbet

The classic Pinakbet is composed of a different assortment of vegetables, including bitter Melon, eggplant, okra, and string beans. Due to the Famous Pinakbet having many different variations, we chose to allow our customers to ask for which kind of our selection of ingredients they wanted us to cook for extra enjoyment and satisfaction. You can specify and customize your pinakbet to your liking, so that our quality chef can cook it especially for you.

P250

Sisig

Sisig is a meaty food containing minced pork with pepper and onions. It's a spicy side dish with a fine and meaty taste, as it is a perfectly balanced dish for your taste buds to enjoy. Our Sisig has been minced to perfection by our specialize cutting machines. For the pork, we chose to once again refer to our quality partnered farms and our high-class chef to specialize and beautify our sisig not just in presentation, but in taste as well.

P150
